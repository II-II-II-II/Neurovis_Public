DATA PRIVACY & AI SECURITY
NeuroVis is a local-first application. Your raw telemetry data never touches our servers.

To utilize the AI Insight Engine, NeuroVis communicates directly with Google's Gemini API from your browser.

Data Masking Protocol:
When you request an analysis, NeuroVis strictly mathematically de-identifies the payload. We only transmit:
• Session duration & classification tag
• Anonymized 30-second biometric averages (HRV, FAA, Beta, AXX)
• Absolutely ZERO Personal Identifiable Information (PII) or raw EEG waveforms are sent.

Bring Your Own Key (BYOK):
To proceed, please enter your personal Gemini API Key. This key is stored securely in your browser's local cache and is never transmitted to us.
